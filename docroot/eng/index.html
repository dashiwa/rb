A significant event is making its return into the athletics world – a match between the teams of Europe and USA. In September of 2019, 300 titled athletes will come to Minsk to compete for the prestigious awards in traditional disciplines of the Queen of the sports and in mixed relays. Each discipline will see eight athletes (4 Europe representatives and 4 athletes from USA). The teams will announce final entry lists on September 1.

This important athletics event will be held at the newly renovated National Olympic stadium “Dinamo”. We are excited to welcome all participants of the intercontinental meet, as well as Belarusian fans!
